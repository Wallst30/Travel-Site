Food

La bandera is a meal of stewed meat over white rice with beans (usually red beans), fried green plantains and salad. ... Popular meats in the Dominican Republic are pork, beef, chicken and even goat. A roast pork dish, known as lechon asado, is particularly popular, as well as cuchifrito, which is a stew of pork innards.
